An Earnest Lasagna

A Lasagna made from the confinements of your Grandma's cottage. Made with the finest pasta dough and the most delectable of tomato sauces.

Ingredients

  • Meat: sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: What do you mean I'm crying, no you are crying!
  • Tomato products: Tomato only found in your grandma's Italian garden.
  • Sugar: A warning of caution, don't mix the salt with the sugar.
  • Spices and seasoning: Such as fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Get the absolute best lasagna noodles you can get! I don't care the means! If you don't, then your grandma would be forever disappointed in you!
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese s'il vous plait.
  • Egg: Egg.

Steps

  1. Conjure up a sauce made with meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer that lasagna up baby!
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving to grandma
  7. Pray and hope grandma likes it
