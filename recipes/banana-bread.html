The Banana Bread of the Ages

A banana bread made from Mount Montgomery in Ohio. Once served to the Duchess of Wales, the Banana Bread of the Ages is an excellent choice to serve for those destined for greatness.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients in one bowl
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl. Add the eggs and mashed bananas.
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in a preheated oven.
